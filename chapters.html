Grow the Network

Start a Chapter

Any city with five or more members in good standing can apply to become a recognized chapter.

Recognized chapters receive a programming stipend, access to shared event resources, and a seat at the national board table. To apply, gather a group of five members and submit a brief proposal outlining planned programming for the first year. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
