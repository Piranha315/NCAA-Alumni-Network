Built by Players, for Players

A community grounded in the game

Every member of this network competed on a varsity collegiate tennis team. That shared experience — early morning practices, conference tournaments, the discipline of balancing academics and competition — forms the bedrock of who we are and why this community works.

Our members span Division I programs at major research universities to tight-knit Division III liberal arts colleges. We represent every graduation year from 1980 through current student-athletes in their final seasons. We are coaches, engineers, lawyers, doctors, founders, and artists. What unites us is the court.

We are alumni-run, which means the people making decisions are the same people who benefit from those decisions. There is no outside management layer — just former players investing time and energy to keep something meaningful alive.

By the Numbers

Our Members at a Glance

A snapshot of who makes up this community.

🎓

Division Breakdown

Approximately 48% D-I, 30% D-II, and 22% D-III alumni — one of the most balanced cross-division networks in collegiate sports.

💼

Career Sectors

Finance, technology, law, medicine, education, and entrepreneurship are the top six sectors our members work in.

🗓️

Graduation Range

Members graduated between 1980 and 2025 — plus early-admit student-athletes through the class of 2028 — with the largest cohorts from classes 2015–2023.
