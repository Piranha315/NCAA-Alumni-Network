Why We Ask

Membership is free. Your support makes that possible.

The Collegiate Tennis Alumni Network operates on an entirely volunteer basis. There is no paid staff. Every dollar contributed goes directly toward programming — event costs, chapter stipends, platform maintenance, and the undergraduate mentorship pipeline.

We keep membership free because we believe cost should never be a barrier to staying connected to the community that shaped you. Voluntary donations from members who are able to contribute make that possible for everyone.

We publish a full financial summary annually. If you'd like to review how contributions have been spent before donating, see the How We're Funded page.

What Your Contribution Funds

Where the money actually goes.

🏆

Annual Tournament

📣

Chapter Stipends

Small annual grants to recognized chapters for local event programming and member outreach.

🎓

Mentorship Program

Coordination costs for matching recent graduates with career mentors across the alumni base.

💻

Platform & Directory

Hosting, domain, and tooling costs for keeping the member directory and this website operational.
