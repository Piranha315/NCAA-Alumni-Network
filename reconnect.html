Pick Up Where You Left Off

Find the people you competed with and against

Life scatters former teammates across cities, industries, and time zones. This section of the network is dedicated to helping you find the people you competed with and against during your collegiate years — and re-establishing those connections in a new context.

Use the member directory to search by city, graduation year, school, or name. When you find someone you know, you can reach out directly through the platform.

We also organize semi-annual "Reconnect" events in major chapters, specifically designed to bring together members who haven't been in touch since their playing days. These gatherings have produced job referrals, business partnerships, and friendships that have lasted years.

Eligibility

Who Can Join

Membership is open to any former varsity collegiate tennis player, regardless of division or graduation year.

  • Competed on a varsity NCAA tennis team (Division I, II, or III)
  • Graduated or departed the program in good standing
  • Current student-athletes in their final year are welcome to apply early
  • International alumni who played at a US collegiate institution are eligible

Not sure if you qualify? Email us and we'll be happy to help.
